Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with Dreamory.

Is Dreamory really free?

+

Yes! Dreamory is completely free to use with no limits on memories, photos, or features. We believe everyone should have access to preserving their life's moments.

What happens to my data?

+

All your memories are stored locally on your device and encrypted. We never have access to your personal memories, photos, or data. You own and control everything.

Can I use it without internet?

+

Absolutely! Dreamory works completely offline. All your memories are stored on your device, so you can capture and view them anytime, anywhere.

How much storage does it use?

+

The app itself is very lightweight. Storage depends on how many photos you add, but we automatically optimize images to save space without losing quality.
