1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect NO personal information. Dreamory is designed to work completely offline without requiring any personal data from you. You don't need to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying information.

Usage Data

We collect NO usage data. We don't track how you use the app, what features you access, or any patterns in your behavior. Your usage of Dreamory is completely private.

Device Information

We may collect basic technical information necessary for the app to function, such as:

  • Device type and operating system version (for app compatibility)
  • Crash reports (only if you choose to share them)
  • App version information

This information is processed locally and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect your personal information, we can't use it. All your memories, photos, ratings, and notes are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Encrypted using industry-standard security
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Fully owned by you and under your complete control

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage systems provided by iOS and Android. Your memories never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export them.

Encryption

We use AES-256 encryption to protect your data on your device. Your memories are encrypted both at rest and during any local processing.

Backup

Your Dreamory data may be included in your device's standard backup systems (iTunes, iCloud, Google Backup). These backups are managed by Apple or Google according to their privacy policies, not ours.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We never share your data because we never have access to it.

Since all your information is stored locally on your device, we cannot and do not:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your information
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Analyze your personal information
  • Access your memories or photos

Legal Compliance

Since we don't collect or store your personal information, there is no data for us to disclose even if legally required to do so.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Complete Control

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device. You can:

  • View all your stored memories at any time
  • Edit or delete any memory
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Delete the app and all data by uninstalling

No Account Required

Since Dreamory doesn't require an account, there are no account settings to manage or data portability requests to make. Your data is already fully portable‚Äîit's on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Dreamory is safe for users of all ages because we don't collect any personal information. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no privacy concerns specific to children under 13.

However, we recommend parental supervision for children using any mobile app, and encourage families to discuss digital privacy and responsible technology use.

7. International Users

Dreamory works the same way regardless of your location. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your privacy is protected equally whether you're in California, Europe, or anywhere else in the world.

8. Future Changes

Cloud Features (If Added)

If we ever add optional cloud sync features in the future, we will:

  • Make them completely optional
  • Update this privacy policy with clear information
  • Implement end-to-end encryption
  • Give you full control over what gets synced
  • Notify you before any changes take effect

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app and update the "Last updated" date above.
