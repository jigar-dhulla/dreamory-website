The Story Behind Dreamory

Dreamory was born from a simple realization: we're living through incredible moments every day, but most of them slip away unnoticed and unremembered.

Our founder started keeping a daily journal but found it time-consuming and hard to maintain. Traditional photo apps felt cluttered and impersonal. Bucket list apps were all about the future, ignoring the amazing things happening right now.

So we built something different‚Äîa personal experience database that captures not just what happened, but how it felt. A place where your bucket list dreams connect to your real-life adventures.
